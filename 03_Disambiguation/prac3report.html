Tagger Comparison

The two taggers I compared were UDPipe and a simple perceptron-based tagger. UDPipe can be found here. UDPipe is a trainable pipeline for tokenizing, tagging, lemmatizing and parsing Universal Treebanks and other CoNLL-U files. The perceptron-based tagger can be found here . It is designed to work with CoNLL-U files and is meant to serve as a teaching aid.
